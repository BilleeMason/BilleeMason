Various foods from each food group is essential in keeping us happy and healthy. Fortunately, most natural foods are free from gluten. And so nature supplies many foods that people can include in their gluten-free diet. These foods come from seven categories: bacteria, yeasts, fungi, algae, plants, animals, and minerals. The abundance of choices is excellent news for people on a restrictive diet.
